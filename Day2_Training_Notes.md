### What is Networking?

Key Concepts:
- Networks allow devices to communicate.
- IP addresses identify devices on a network.
- Routers direct traffic between networks.
- The internet is a collection of interconnected networks.

What I Learned:
- Networking is the foundation of cybersecurity.
- Understanding how devices communicate is essential for both defensive and offensive security.
- Penetration testers need strong networking knowledge.
